HOW TO PLAY
AIM & SHOOT
Move your mouse to aim at the bubble grid. Click anywhere on the screen to fire.
MATCH & POP
Connect 3 or more same-colored bubbles to pop them. Larger groups score more points. Disconnected clusters fall and score bonus points!
SPECIAL BUBBLES
● BOMB — Blasts all bubbles within a radius
● LASER — Clears the entire row instantly
● RAINBOW — Wildcard, matches any color
● ARMOR — Requires 2 hits to destroy
COMBOS
Pop bubbles quickly to build a combo multiplier up to 3×. The faster you chain pops, the higher your score!
WIN CONDITION
Clear all bubbles before they reach the red line at the bottom. You have a limited number of shots — earn up to 3 stars based on accuracy. Complete all 40 levels to conquer Dropzone!
